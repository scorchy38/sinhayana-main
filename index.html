Welcome to Sinhayana

We are here to help everyone in need

Initiated in 2016, Sinhayana Foundation is an independent, non-profit organization that strongly believes in a ‘community-first approach.’ Built on the principles of social justice, equity and empathy, Sinhayana Foundation believes in creating deep impact within communities that are systematically marginalized. So far, we have worked with urban slum dwelling communities that largely comprise families of migrant workers who are employed in the informal sector, and have extremely limited access to basic resources for survival.
